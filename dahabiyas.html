VEDA 1

This beautiful 50meters dahabiya offers a peaceful organic platform to host your custom made holidays.

ROOMS AND SUITES

VEDA 1 can host up to 20 people . It has 10 rooms with double beds which can be converted into single beds. Each room has  a private bathroom and a little desk. 
4 rooms are suites with a nice balcony. 

COMMON AREAS

Two air conditioned meeting space for up to 50 people. 
Upper deck meeting room 360 degree panoramic view. 
Several specifically designed spaces for relaxation, to have workshops or yoga / meditation.
Nice library with a collection of Egyptian history and awareness books
